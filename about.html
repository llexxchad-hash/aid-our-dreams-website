About Aid Our Dreams

Helping young people who are left to figure life out alone — through education, mentorship, and career guidance since 2020.

Who We Are

A Team Driven by Purpose

Aid Our Dreams is a non-profit organization founded to help young people who are left to figure life out alone. We support children and youth through education, mentorship, and career guidance, helping them make better choices and believe that their future is worth fighting for.

Aid Our Dreams was founded in March 2020. Our first project was held in December 2020, where we distributed school supplies to children to support their education. Our second project took place on June 16, 2021, when we celebrated African Child's Day with students from different schools. During this event, students were taught important lessons and positively impacted through educational and motivational activities.

Since its establishment in 2020, Aid Our Dreams has remained committed to supporting and inspiring young people to believe in themselves and pursue a better future.
